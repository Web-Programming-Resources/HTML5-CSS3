LuxAir Slashes Prices

LuxAir announced this week that it is cutting roundtrip airfare from New York to Luxembourg by 20%. Travelers can now travel to Luxembourg for $850. The price cut will good for travel up to June 28th. Other international carriers are expected to follow suit, taking part in what will be the first major fare war of the summer.
